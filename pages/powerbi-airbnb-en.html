New York City Airbnb Data Analysis


Link para o Painel Online: Visão Geral

1. Overview

The report developed in this project examines the available accommodations market in New York City, using Airbnb data provided via Kaggle. The main goal is to identify trends and patterns in the characteristics of listed properties, such as accommodation type, location, average price, and user reviews. Based on this information, dashboards were developed in Power BI that offer valuable insights for strategic decision-making, such as price adjustments, service quality improvements, and market opportunity identification.

2. Business Problem

Airbnb operates in a dynamic and highly competitive market, which features a wide variety of accommodations with distinct characteristics and a demand that is constantly evolving among travelers. The lack of accurate insights about the market situation hampers strategic decision-making, such as price adjustments, service optimization, and market opportunity identification.

3. Solution

The report developed in this project using the Power BI tool offers a solution to this problem by providing a comprehensive and detailed view of the accommodation market in New York. It has two main views:

  1. Overall Metrics: This page provides an overview of the property landscape in New York City. Here, we can check data on the number of reviews and the average price per neighborhood and district, along with other information.

  2. Metrics Evolution: On this page, we can see the variation of metrics over time and also the geographical location of the properties.

Important: In this analysis, we assume the following hypotheses:

  • The date of the first review of an accommodation corresponds to the date the property was listed on Airbnb by the owner.
  • All customers provided a review, with the review date corresponding to the check-in date (reviews = bookings).

4. Insights

In this report, the following insights were identified:

  1. The highest number of bookings occurs during the summer months in the Northern Hemisphere, particularly during the school vacation period in June and July.

  2. The district of Manhattan is the most expensive for all three types of accommodations.

  3. Shared rooms receive fewer average reviews compared to other types of accommodations.

5. Future Enhancements

Possible enhancements for the Power BI report include:

  1. Integration with real-time data sources for updated insights.

  2. Deriving new features to make the analysis more precise.
