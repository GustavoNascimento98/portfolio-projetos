1. Overview

The main objective of this project is to create and publish an interactive Power BI dashboard using a fictional e-commerce dataset called of a company called TUDO AQUI. This dataset, provided and downloaded from Kaggle, was utilized during the Introduction to Power BI course conducted at Comunidade DS.

2. Business Problem

You were hired by the company TUDO AQUI as a Junior Data Analyst.

TUDO AQUI, a growing Brazilian e-commerce firm, has historically relied on intuitive decision-making by its management team, resulting in occasional losses due to a lack of thorough data analysis. Recognizing the need for more informed decision-making, the CEO has embarked on implementing Power BI to leverage business intelligence. This strategic shift aims to move the company away from intuition towards a data-driven approach, fostering more accurate and beneficial decision-making processes.

To facilitate this transition, the CEO has decided to tackle one analytical area at a time, beginning with Product, Payments, Orders, Reviews, Sellers, and finally, Sales. Each area will undergo detailed analysis to uncover insights crucial for informed decision-making. Initially, the focus will be on developing and delivering these analyses using Power BI Desktop, ensuring a systematic and effective approach to leveraging data for business growth.
